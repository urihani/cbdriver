What's CBD ?

CBD (“Cannabidiol”) is a component of cannabis that can be helpful for medical use.

- Cannabis is composed of CBD and THC which is the component that make it illegal.

- In medical use, CBD has an anxiolytic effect that can help to fight anxiety disorders.

- Some studies prove that CBD can also help to cure schizophrenia and Parkinson's disease.
